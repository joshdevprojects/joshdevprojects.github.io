    Welcome to my website.

    My name is Katja Hajek and I am a Clinical Psychologist. I have an MA and PhD degree in Psychology, and I have worked for 25 years for the NHS (South London and Maudsley NHS Trust) as a Consultant Clinical Psychologist.

    My specialism was adult mental health and inpatient services. During this time I worked with patients suffering with depression, severe anxiety, bipolar disorder and other issues, such as psychosis and addiction. Apart from my NHS work, I have also developed a private practice, and this work continues now that I have retired from the NHS. I mainly see clients with anxiety, work related stress, relationship/marital problems, lack of confidence etc. I see individual clients as well as couples, and I am especially interested in helping couples to resolve their problems and improve their relationships.

    With my extensive experience I believe I am able to assess clients' problems quickly, and tailor the therapy to the nature of the problem. I am a good listener, I am non-judgemental and approachable.
